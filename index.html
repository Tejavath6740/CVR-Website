CVR College of Engineering is a private autonomous engineering institution located at Mangalpalli (Vastunagar), Hyderabad, Telangana. It was established in 2001 by the Cherabuddi Education Society. The college is approved by AICTE, affiliated to JNTUH, and has autonomous status.
